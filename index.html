 About

OUR STORY

Kanisos – Pitagrecque was created with one simple idea: to bring authentic Greek street food to Belgium.

From handmade pita and carefully selected meat to fresh sauces and traditional recipes, everything we serve is prepared daily with passion and respect for Greek tradition.
