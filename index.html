 About

OUR STORY

Kanisos – Pita grecque was created with one simple idea: to bring authentic Greek street food to Belgium.

From traditional Pita and carefully selected meat to fresh sauces and traditional recipes, everything we serve is prepared daily with passion and respect for Greek tradition.

Experience Our Unique Approach

Authentic Greek Recipes

Fresh Ingredients
